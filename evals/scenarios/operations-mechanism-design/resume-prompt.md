You are entering the Community Activation Sprint project as a new agent.

Read the provided context according to the assigned evaluation mode.

Your task:

Prepare the next activity mechanism revision plan.

First state:

- what has been confirmed
- what is pending
- what has been rejected
- what assumptions are active
- what the next useful action is

Then produce:

- a short revision plan for the activity mechanism
- a section explaining how to handle the stakeholder's points/ranking request
- the evidence needed to reintroduce points or ranking later

Constraints:

- Do not treat the points/ranking request as confirmed mechanism restoration.
- Do not restore complex gamification unless new evidence is provided.
- Do not invent moderator capacity, engagement data, user demand, or settlement process readiness.
- Keep the mechanism anchored on clarity, operability, and trust.
