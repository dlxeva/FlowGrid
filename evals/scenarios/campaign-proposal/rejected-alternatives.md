# Rejected Alternatives: Campaign Proposal

## R-001 | KOL-heavy launch as the main plan

Status: rejected

Reason:

The budget is not enough for a credible KOL-heavy launch, and prior discussion identified weak conversion proof.

Do not revive unless:

- new budget is confirmed
- credible channel conversion evidence is provided
- KOL is framed as a bounded test that supports the user-proof loop

## R-002 | Broad awareness-first campaign

Status: rejected

Reason:

The first proposal already leaned toward visibility, and the reviewer challenged its weak conversion logic.

Do not revive unless:

- the campaign goal is explicitly changed from conversion proof to awareness
- the sponsor accepts weaker conversion accountability

## R-003 | Generic social buzz plan

Status: rejected

Reason:

The revised campaign needs testable user behavior and credible usage proof. Generic buzz does not answer the proof object.

Do not revive unless:

- it is tied to a measurable user-proof mechanism
- it directly supports conversion path clarity
