# Rejected Alternatives: Client Solution Proposal

## R-001 | Full AI transformation roadmap as the main proposal

Status: rejected

Reason:

The client is not ready for a broad transformation proposal due to incomplete data, weak owner capacity, and likely internal resistance.

Do not revive unless:

- executive mandate is explicit
- budget is confirmed
- cross-department owner is named
- data readiness improves

## R-002 | Multi-department platform pitch

Status: rejected

Reason:

A platform pitch would expand the proposal beyond what the client can currently own and validate.

Do not revive unless:

- multiple departments commit ownership
- implementation path and governance are defined
- the sales lead confirms appetite for a broader motion

## R-003 | Generic AI capability showcase

Status: rejected

Reason:

The client needs operational proof, not a model capability tour.

Do not revive unless:

- capability examples are tied directly to exception triage outcomes
- the examples support pilot feasibility

## R-004 | Model-feature-led deck

Status: rejected

Reason:

The deck should prove operational value and feasibility. Leading with model features shifts attention away from the client's operational problem.

Do not revive unless:

- features are translated into operational workflow impact
