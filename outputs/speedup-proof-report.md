## What changed

The closeout path now segments the transcript once for the original-text extractors and once for the code-stripped decision extractor, then reuses those lists across the pipeline.

**Why behavior remains equivalent:** The cached lists use the same segmentation and code-stripping operations as before; direct extractor calls retain their original one-argument behavior, and normalized before/after patch sections matched with a fixed hash seed.

| Complexity | Before | After |
|---|---:|---:|
| Estimate | Repeated full transcript segmentation across seven extractors plus repeated context-window segmentation. | Two full transcript segmentations per closeout, with shared segment lists for all extractors and decision relation inference. |
