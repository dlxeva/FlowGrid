# Session Transcript: AI Product Launch Campaign

## Meeting Notes - 2026-06-24

### Background

The client (TechCorp) wants to launch their new AI product. They said "we want to do a launch campaign" but haven't specified what that means.

### Discussion

**User:** The client wants to do an AI product launch. What should we focus on?

**AI:** Let's start by defining the problem. What is the product? Who is the target user?

**User:** It's an AI writing assistant for enterprise users. The target is mid-size companies.

**AI:** Good. Now let's think about the goals. What does success look like?

**User:** We need to generate awareness and get 1000 trial users in the first month.

**AI:** That's a clear goal. What about non-goals?

**User:** We're not doing paid ads in this phase. Focus on organic and PR.

**AI:** Understood. What are the constraints?

**User:** Budget is 50k RMB. Timeline is 6 weeks. We have 2 people on the team.

### Key Decisions

**User:** Let's decide on the approach. We'll focus on content marketing and influencer partnerships.

**AI:** That makes sense given the constraints. Should we define the user personas?

**User:** Yes, we need to do that. The primary user is a marketing manager at a 100-500 person company.

**AI:** What about the review criteria? How will the client evaluate success?

**User:** They want to see the campaign plan, content calendar, and KOL list. We'll present in 2 weeks.

### Risks

**User:** There's a risk that the KOLs we want are too expensive.

**AI:** Good point. We should have a backup list of micro-influencers.

**User:** Another risk is that the product positioning isn't clear yet.

**AI:** We should schedule a call with the client to clarify the USP.

### Next Steps

**User:** Next step is to create a detailed project plan.

**AI:** I'll draft a timeline with milestones.

**User:** We also need to confirm the budget allocation.

**AI:** Let's break down the 50k into content, KOLs, and PR.

### Summary

We've decided to focus on content marketing and KOL partnerships. The goal is 1000 trial users in 6 weeks with a 50k budget. Next steps include creating a project plan and confirming budget allocation.
